Legally Binding Agreement

Effective Upon Mention

Article I - Agreement to Terms

By mentioning me on Discord, herein referred to as "The Enforcer," you, herein referred to as "The Mentioner," explicitly and irrevocably agree to the terms outlined in this legally binding contract.

Article II - Obligations of The Mentioner

Upon invoking The Enforcer's username in any manner within the Discord platform, The Mentioner forfeits all rights to their display name and profile picture. The Enforcer shall retain absolute and unquestionable authority to dictate, modify, and enforce changes to The Mentioner’s display name and profile picture at will.

Article III - Duration and Enforcement

This agreement remains in effect for six (6) months from the moment of mention. During this period, The Mentioner waives all rights to contest, appeal, or negotiate any decisions made by The Enforcer regarding their display name or profile picture.

Article IV - No Recourse or Escape

The Mentioner acknowledges that there exists no legal, ethical, or moral recourse to challenge this agreement while it remains in effect. Any attempt to do so will result in further punitive alterations to The Mentioner’s profile, at the sole discretion of The Enforcer.

Article V - Expiration and Renewal

Upon expiration of the six (6) month term, The Mentioner may regain control of their display name and profile picture unless they mention The Enforcer again, at which point a new six (6) month term will immediately take effect.

Article VI - Final Clause

By mentioning The Enforcer, The Mentioner voluntarily, knowingly, and willingly submits to the absolute rule of The Enforcer regarding their online identity. There shall be no exceptions, amendments, or mercy.

Signed in perpetuity,
The Enforcer
